WHO WE ARE

The VMMS was introduced by VMMS sri lanka, a decade ago, to address the dearth of after sales service providers for automobiles that are imported to Sri Lanka by independent vehicle importers.

WHAT WE DO

A regular service schedule can help keep your car running its best. For your convenience, VMMS has developed a service interval schedule to help you keep track of regular maintenance.

VISION

To be the best and the most trusted vehicle supplier and aftercare service provider in Sri Lanka.

MISSION

Driven by the principles of honesty and dedication, we strive to offer Sri Lankans affordable ‘peoples’ vehicles’, while providing services that exceed their expectations.

STERLING AFTERCARE GUARANTEE

The VMMS Aftercare Guarantee is issued by VMMS sri lanka for the vehicles that it imports to Sri Lanka. The vehicles that are exported to the Sri Lanka by VMMS are distributed through a network of more than 200 auto dealers in the country.
